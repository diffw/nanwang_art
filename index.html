About

Nan Wang, born in the 1980s in Changsha, Hunan. Christian, designer, husband to one woman, father of two children. Started exploring the internet during university in 2001, fascinated by website design and Flash animation, fortunate to experience the most free era of China's internet. Transitioned to App design in 2009, one of China's earliest mobile internet designers. From 2013 to 2019, as the 7th employee and first designer, fully participated in Liulishuo's journey from early startup in a residential apartment to IPO on NYSE (NYSE: LAIX). Previously lived in Hangzhou and Shanghai. Currently resides in Dallas, Texas, USA.
